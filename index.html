Welcome to Colombo

The capital of Sri Lanka

Colombo is the largest city in Sri Lanka. It has many colonial buildings. The city is located on the west coast of sri lanka, in the western province basically.

Famous places:

  • Galle Face Green
  • Colombo National Museum
  • Gangaramaya temple
  • Colombo also has really good markets and street food.

    • Colombo has a tropical climate and is warm and humid most of the year. Tourists often visit during the dry season.
